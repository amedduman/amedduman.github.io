Intro

I am an Unity game developer who has 4 plus years of experience. I am extremely motivated to constantly develop my skills and grow professionally. I am confident in my ability to create awesome games. You can checkout my works here.

I have studied Digital Game Design at Istanbul Bilgi University. I started to work as an unity game developer when I was in the 3rd year of university at a game studio which targets mobile game sector.
